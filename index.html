About Me

Hello! I am a freelance technology professional with expertise in product management, QA testing, IT support, and web development. With a strong foundation in networking, operating systems, and cybersecurity, I have worked across various domains, helping businesses enhance their digital infrastructure and optimize workflows.

My journey began as a Desktop Support Engineer at IBM Ahmedabad, followed by a role in IT support for Ayushman Bharat in Diu. Over the years, I have gained hands-on experience in API testing, software quality assurance, and project management, utilizing tools like Postman, Swagger, Jira, ClickUp, and Trello to streamline processes and improve efficiency.

In addition to my technical experience, I have also contributed to education as a contractual lecturer for HCL and Times of India, where I trained students in networking, operating systems, and Linux.

Currently, I am focused on expanding my skills in web development and DevOps, continuously learning and adapting to the evolving technology landscape.

Beyond technology, I have a passion for farming and organic cultivation, which allows me to connect with nature and explore sustainable agricultural practices.

When I'm not coding, you can find me farming, cycling, or sharing experiences with locals. I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow, so feel free to reach out!
